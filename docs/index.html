Proteinx Interaction Networks/PPINs

understanding PPINs is crucial for studying cell physiology in normal/disease states. It is also essential in drug development.

Koncept

Protein–protein interactions (PPIs) are the physical contacts of high specificity established between two or more protein molecules as a result of biochemical events steered by electrostatic forces including the hydrophobic effect.

Protein-protein interaction networks (PPINs) are mathematical representations of the physical contacts between proteins in the cell.
